How It Works (Directive Flow)

  • Facts – Cart items (qty, price, maxStock), coupon code/status/discount, checkout status/error, auth state
  • Derivations – subtotal, itemCount, isEmpty, discount, tax, total, hasOverstockedItem, freeShipping (all auto-tracked)
  • Constraints – quantityLimit (80), couponValidation (70), checkoutReady (60, after: ['quantityLimit', 'couponValidation'], cross-module auth dep)
  • Resolvers – adjustQuantity (clamps to stock), validateCoupon (500ms mock), processCheckout (retry 2x exponential, 10% failure)
  • Cross-Module – Cart's checkoutReady constraint depends on auth.isAuthenticated via crossModuleDeps

Constraints declare what's needed, resolvers handle the async work. The checkout constraint waits for quantity and coupon constraints to settle first.
