How It Works (Directive Flow)

  • Facts – pool, table, removed, selected, moveCount, gameOver, message
  • Derivations – poolCount, removedCount, selectedTiles, hasValidMoves (all auto-tracked)
  • Constraints – pairAddsTen (100), refillTable (50), noMovesLeft (190), allCleared (200)
  • Resolvers – removeTiles (multi-fact mutation), refillTable, endGame
  • Events – newGame, selectTile, deselectTile, clearSelection

Constraints fire by priority: a matching pair triggers pairAddsTen → removeTiles resolver. Then refillTable fires to replenish the grid from the pool. When no valid moves remain, noMovesLeft ends the game.
