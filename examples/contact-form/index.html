How It Works (Directive Flow)

  • Facts – name, email, subject, message, touched, status, errorMessage, lastSubmittedAt, submissionCount
  • Derivations – nameError, emailError, subjectError, messageError, isValid, canSubmit, messageCharCount (all auto-tracked)
  • Constraints – submitForm (status === “submitting”), resetAfterSuccess (status === “success”)
  • Resolvers – sendMessage (simulated async submit, 20% fail rate), resetAfterDelay (3s auto-reset)
  • Effects – logSubmission (logs status transitions)

Validation derivations re-compute automatically when any field changes. The canSubmit derivation composes isValid with status and rate-limit checks. Constraints trigger the resolver chain: submit → success → auto-reset.
