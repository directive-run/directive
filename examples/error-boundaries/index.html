How It Works (Directive Flow)

  • Circuit Breaker – CLOSED → OPEN after 3 failures, auto-recovers to HALF_OPEN after 5s
  • Retry-Later – exponential backoff (1s, 2s, 4s) with max 3 retries
  • Performance Plugin – tracks resolver latency, reconciliation cost, slow operation warnings
