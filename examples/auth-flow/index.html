How It Works (Directive Flow)

  • Facts – email, password, token, refreshToken, expiresAt, user, status, now (ticking timer)
  • Derivations – isAuthenticated, isExpiringSoon, canRefresh, tokenTimeRemaining, canLogin (all auto-tracked)
  • Constraints – needsLogin (100), refreshNeeded (90), needsUser (80, after: ['refreshNeeded'])
  • Resolvers – login, refreshToken (retry 2x exponential), fetchUser
  • Effects – logStatusChange logs transitions to the timeline

The after ordering ensures user profile is fetched with a fresh token. The ticking now fact drives reactive expiry detection.
