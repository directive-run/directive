How It Works (Directive Flow)

  • Facts – url, status, connectRequested, messages, retryCount, maxRetries, now (ticking timer)
  • Derivations – isConnected, shouldReconnect, reconnectDelay, reconnectCountdown, canSend, messageCount (all auto-tracked)
  • Constraints – needsConnection (100) fires on connect, needsReconnect (90) fires on error with exponential backoff
  • Resolvers – connect creates MockWebSocket, reconnect waits backoff delay then retriggers connection
  • Effects – logStatusChange logs transitions to the timeline

The reconnect constraint drives automatic retry with exponential backoff. The ticking now fact drives the reactive countdown display.
