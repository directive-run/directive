How It Works (Directive Flow)

  • Two Modules – filters owns search/sort/category; list owns items, cursor, and pagination state
  • crossModuleDeps – list declares a dependency on filters for type-safe cross-module access in constraints and effects
  • filterChanged constraint – hashes filter values and compares to lastFilterHash; when different, emits RESET_AND_LOAD
  • loadMore constraint – fires when hasMore && !isLoadingMore && scrollNearBottom (three conditions prevent duplicate fetches)
  • IntersectionObserver effect – watches a sentinel element at the bottom of the list; sets scrollNearBottom when it enters the viewport
  • Resolvers – loadPage appends items; resetAndLoad clears and re-fetches page 1 with new filters

Filter values are passed through the requirement payload so resolvers stay module-scoped. The loggingPlugin logs every constraint evaluation and resolver execution to the console.
